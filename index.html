About

From graphic design, to web development, to game design, I am passionate about creating work that engages people. Currently majoring in Interactive Media Design at the University of Washington Bothell, I continuously aim to pursue a higher understanding of what it takes to create a successful product. For me, design isn't about just aesthetics; it's about finding creative solutions to problems in a way that can create a positive impact in the world. Starting from the conceptual phases to completion, I believe in giving each project time, care, and understanding no matter how small.
